About Me

My name is Taneli Manninen. I am a 24-year-old student studying for a degree in engineering with a focus on information and communication technology at Jyväskylä University of Applied Sciences (JAMK).

I am currently on my fourth and final year of studies. I specialize in application development and digital media.

As a highly creative person, I am naturally inspired by all things visual. User-centricity and usability particularly fascinate me in software development, and I believe they’re an integral aspect of the whole process.

This spring, my thesis work focused on the UI/UX aspect of a React Native mobile application project.

I just moved to Helsinki to get a fresh change in life. I'm actively searching for an internship/junior position in the Helsinki metropolitan area and really excited to possibly enter the working life.

Values

I highly value individual initiative, perseverance, proactivity, and of course, creativity. I strive to develop these qualities in myself on a daily basis.

I constantly practice and improve my personal qualities in my free time, for example, through my music hobby. I play in a band and produce my own small projects for social media, where my creativity and energy can "bloom," so to speak.
