Future Factory - Project

In the spring of 2022, I participated in the production team of JAMK's Future Factory project. Our team's assignment was to produce a Forum solution as a service, to be integrated into the existing website of our client, WIMMA Lab educational environment.

The project lasted for approximately 2.5 months. The end result was an easy-to-use and stylish website.

During the project, I worked as a Web Developer / Frontend Developer. I was mainly responsible for the design, styling, and functionality of the Forum website. I also styled and built our team's own website.

I was heavily involved in project management and documentation during the project. For example, I maintained our team's weekly status update, wrote team introductions and plans.

Military Service

In 2019, I served as a conscript for 6 months in Kajaani, Finland in the Headquarters Company of the Signal Battalion. My service began in January and ended in July of the same year.

My military rank was a private, and I worked as a signalman. However, I received specialized training as a tracked vehicle driver, which I drove in exercises in challenging forest terrain.

The army taught and strengthened many useful qualities in me as a person. The experience gave me perseverance, enthusiasm, taught me to take responsibility and courage to face problems! Of course, it also provided me with many dear memories!
